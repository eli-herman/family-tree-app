## Cross-Phase Integration Issues

1. **profile.tsx → familyStore**: Profile tab reads `mockFamilyMembers[0]` (hardcoded "Peggy") instead of using `useFamilyStore.getMemberById(currentMemberId)`. Breaks DATA-03.

2. **authStore → userStore**: No mapping from Firebase Auth UID to family member ID. `userStore.currentMemberId` is hardcoded to `'eli'`. Will break for any other authenticated user.

3. **subscriptionStore → screens**: `FeatureGate` and `UpgradeBanner` are built and exported but imported nowhere. Free tier limits are never enforced.

4. **feedStore → member profiles**: `getItemsByAuthor` selector exists and is ready but not called in `member/[id].tsx`. Member posts are invisible.
